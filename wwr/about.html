White Water Rafting

Our purpose is to ignite a passion for adventure and foster a deep respect for nature by providing thrilling, unforgettable rafting experiences. Guided by our mission to ensure safety, skill, and stewardship on every journey, we believe that the river has something to teach everyone who dares to ride its currents. With the motto, Ride the Rapids, Live the Adventure, we stand by our creed: adventure with purpose, safety as a promise, and respect for the wild as our guiding force.

History

Founded in 1998 by a group of passionate river guides and outdoor enthusiasts, Rapids & Trails Adventure Co. began as a small operation along the rushing waters of the Colorado River. What started as a few friends sharing their love of the river with adventurous souls has since evolved into a premier white water rafting company, known for its commitment to safety, environmental stewardship, and exhilarating experiences. Over the years, our team has expanded to include expert guides with deep knowledge of the region’s waterways and ecosystems, and we have proudly introduced thousands of guests to the thrill of navigating rapids while fostering a connection to the natural world. Today, we continue to honor our roots, blending tradition and innovation to bring unforgettable river adventures to life.
