All possible trips you can take with us

Trip Name Location Description
Royal Gorge Colorado Experience adrenaline-pumping Class IV and V rapids through a canyon of towering rock walls and dramatic scenery on the Arkansas River.
Ocoee River Tennessee Conquer the thrilling whitewater of this Olympic course, known for its continuous rapids, crystal-clear water, and vibrant Appalachian scenery.
Salmon River Idaho Dive into the “River of No Return” for exhilarating rapids, serene stretches, and breathtaking views of Idaho’s rugged wilderness.
Chattooga River Georgia/South Carolina Tackle the Southeast’s iconic wild river with a mix of thrilling rapids, pristine forests, and stunning waterfalls.
Youghiogheny River Pennsylvania Ride the frothy waves of the Lower Yough for a family-friendly yet exciting whitewater experience in the heart of the Laurel Highlands.
Colorado River Grand Canyon, Arizona Take on legendary rapids surrounded by the awe-inspiring grandeur of the Grand Canyon, a bucket-list adventure for any thrill-seeker.
Nantahala River North Carolina Paddle through the misty, cool waters of the Nantahala Gorge, perfect for first-time rafters and scenic enthusiasts alike.
American River California Charge through the roaring rapids of the South Fork, a favorite among adventurers seeking high-energy fun in the Sierra Nevada foothills.
Snake River Wyoming Journey through the rugged beauty of Jackson Hole, with thrilling rapids and panoramic views of the Teton Mountain Range.
Deschutes River Oregon Experience the perfect blend of splashy rapids and calm stretches, ideal for adventurers of all levels in Oregon’s high desert landscape.
